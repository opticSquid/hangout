1. [[Creator]] Definition and Affiliation
	1. **[[Creator]]s** are a specific type of **[[End User]]** on the platform
	2. They can affiliate with a **[[Platform Partner]]** in two ways: on a per-post basis, or for a fixed duration under a revenue-sharing model.
2. Benefits for [[Platform Partner]]s
	1. **[[Platform Partner]]s** are motivated to affiliate with **[[Creator]]s** because it allows them to access distinct customer bases all at once.
	2. Each **[[Creator]]** typically has a niche, meaning their audience often has a predefined profile. This enables businesses to precisely choose which customer segment they want to target.
	3. In the creator marketplace, **[[Platform Partner]]s** will be able to view the audience analytics of **[[Creator]]s** to help inform their decisions.
