## Intelligent Personalization for [[End User]]s
The app's core strength lies in its ability to **continuously learn and refine recommendations** for **[[End User]]s**. We'll achieve this by leveraging various data points:
1. **User Details & Preferences:** Information provided by the **[[End User]]** about their tastes and what they're looking for.
2. **Usage Behavior:** How the **[[End User]]** interacts with the app, including what they view, click on, and skip.
3. **[[Purchase History]]:** Past bookings and services availed by the **[[End User]]**.
4. **Prompt History:** The types of queries and inputs the **[[End User]]** has made to the app.
5. **Location History:** Places the **[[End User]]** has visited or shown interest in.

Optionally, we might also use device details to get a rough estimate of an **[[End User]]'s** purchasing power, allowing us to further tailor recommendations.

## Empowering [[Platform Partner]]s with Insights

We can **aggregate all this collected data from [[End User]]s** and present it to **[[Platform Partner]]s** in an easily understandable format. It's crucial to note that this data will **never include any Personally Identifiable Information (PII)** of the **[[End User]]s**. This aggregated, anonymized data will be a powerful tool for **[[Platform Partner]]s** to gain insights into customer preferences and trends, ultimately helping them **improve their business strategies** and offerings.
