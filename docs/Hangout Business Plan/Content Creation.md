## For [[Creator]]s
This section focuses on the tools and support provided to **[[Creator]]s** to help them produce engaging content and build their brand.
1. Mobile-First Content Creation Tools
	1. [[Creator]]s will receive mobile-first video editing and content creation tools. These tools are designed to help them easily create content about their experiences using a **[[Platform Partner]]'s** service.
	2. They'll have access to user-friendly video editing features, including AI-recommended transitions that seamlessly match the scene.
2. Content Management & Performance
	1. Creators can edit and re-post videos at any time. However, the original post date won't change, and the algorithm will favor active accounts with more recent, highly engaging posts.
	2. [Creator]]s will also be able to view the performance of their content, including demographics and various analytical metrics. This helps them understand their **[[End User]]s** better.
3. Brand Building
	1. These features and insights will empower [[Creator]]s to establish their personal brand and connect more effectively with their audience.

## For [[Platform Partner]]s
This section outlines how **[[Platform Partner]]s** leverage content creation and analytics for business growth and brand building, incorporating all the tools available to [[Creator]]s.
1. Comprehensive Tool set
	1. **[[Platform Partner]]s** will have access to all the same content creation and editing tools provided to **[[Creator]]s**.
2. Enhanced Performance Analytics
	1. They will be able to see the performance metrics for their own videos.
	2. Additionally, they'll gain insights into the performance of specific videos posted by their affiliated **[[Creator]]s** about their business.
3. Data-Driven Decision Making & Branding
	1. Collectively, these data points will enable **[[Platform Partner]]s** to make informed decisions and strategically cultivate their brand perception among **[[End User]]s**.
