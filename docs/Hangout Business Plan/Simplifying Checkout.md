## Streamlining the Checkout Experience for [[End User]]s

**Simplifying the process for an [[End User]] to avail a service from a [[Platform Partner]] is paramount** for our business. This streamlined experience will be the primary driver of revenue. The easier, more reliable, and consistent the checkout process is across all **[[Platform Partner]]s**, the more trust the platform will build among **[[End User]]s**.
Here's how we envision the simplified checkout flow:
1. **Direct Call to Action:** Every post will prominently feature a button allowing the **[[End User]]** to directly avail the service offered by the **[[Platform Partner]]**.
2. **Service Details Collection:** The next page will efficiently gather any specific details required by that particular **[[Platform Partner]]** from the **[[End User]]** to provide the service.
3. **Secure Payment:** The third step will be the dedicated payment page.
4. **Confirmation & Navigation:** Following successful payment confirmation, a page will display route information, guiding the **[[End User]]** from their current location to the **[[Platform Partner]]'s** location.
5. **Successful Purchase:** This entire sequence will constitute a successful purchase.
6. **Purchase History:** A record of all these purchases will be easily accessible and viewable under the **[[Purchase History]]** page for the **[[End User]]**.
