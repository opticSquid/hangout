## For [[End User]]s
The core of this application is providing a seamless and personalized experience for **[[End User]]s** looking for places to hang out.
1. Initial Recommendations & Search
	1. The app will recommend places to hang out, taking into account the [[End User]]'s taste and budget. [[Empathizing with User]]
	2. The app would integrate with an LLM model to take user's queries in plain English and show related recommendations. [[Empathizing with User]]
2. Location-Based Discovery & Navigation
	1. The recommendations would prioritize physical closeness to the user's location but this behavior should be modifiable. [[Finding Places]]
	2. The users will be shown location of the [[Platform Partner]] in the map and navigation information to the place once they checkout. [[End User Experience]]
3. Engaging User Interface
	1. The app would be mobile first, focused on short form videos and should be swipe friendly. The UI would be like Instagram Reels or TikTok or YouTube Shorts. [[End User Experience]]
4. Personalization & Interaction
	1. The app would be able to learn from user's usage pattern and order history and personalize recommendations. [[Personalization]]
	2. The [[End User]]s would be able to heart, comment, share posts made by either [[Creator]]s or [[Platform Partner]]s themselves.
5. Simplified Transactions
	1. The user will be able to purchase the services of the [[Platform Partner]] directly from the posts created on the platform. [[Simplifying Checkout]]
6. Core Principles for End User Experience
	1. The app would prioritize ease of usage and user experience for [[End User]] above anything else. [[End User Experience]]
	2. The app would be focused on doing one thing and one thing only and doing it well than any one else. [[End User Experience]]

## For [[Creator]]s
**[[Creator]]s** play a vital role in enriching the content ecosystem, building upon all the capabilities available to **[[End User]]s**.
1. Foundation
	1. All capabilities of [[End User]] is included here.
2. Content Creation & Sharing
	1. The [[Creator]]s would also be able to make content about a service of the [[Platform Partner]] they availed describing their experience given they booked the service of the [[Platform Partner]] through our app. And the other [[End User]]s, [[Creator]]s and [[Platform Partner]]s would be able to view and interact with this content. [[Content Creation]]
3. Community & Engagement
	1. [[End User]]s would be able to follow a [[Creator]]. And [[End User]]s would be notified when their followed creators release new content.
4. Creator Support & Analytics
	1. The app should also help [[Creator]]s to create better content also help them see how their content is performing and guide them on how to improve it. [[Content Creation]]
5. Monetization for Creators
	1. The only thing that differentiates a [[Creator]] from a [[Platform Partner]] is a [[Creator]] can not sell their own service. They can only sell services of a [[Platform Partner]].
	2. The [[Creator]] will be paid per [[End User]] who avails the service of [[Platform Partner]] being advertised through their post.

## For [[Platform Partner]]s
**[[Platform Partner]]s** are businesses that utilize the platform to reach [[End User]]s and optimize their operations, encompassing all the capabilities of a **[[Creator]]**.
1. Foundation
	1. All capabilities of [[Creator]] is included here.
2. Business Intelligence & Optimization:
	1. The app should be able to provide them the aggregated data of the [[End User]]s of our platform without any PII information. [[Business Intelligence Tools]]
	2. The app should help them meaningfully to optimize their business to the taste and budget of the [[End User]]s coming to their place. [[Business Intelligence Tools]]
	3. The app should also help find more customer segments to expand to scale their business. [[Business Intelligence Tools]]
3. Content Management & Affiliation
	1. The app should also help them see how their own content or their affiliated [[Creator]]'s content is performing and also help them understand how that content performance is translating into customer footfall to show tangible results. [[Content Creation]] [[Business Intelligence Tools]]
	2. The app would help them find suitable [[Creator]]s to affiliate to in case the [[Platform Partner]] does not want to create their own content or wants to promote themselves through [[Creator Market Place]].
