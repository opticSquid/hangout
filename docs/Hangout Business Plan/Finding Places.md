## Proximity and Preference: Tailoring [[Platform Partner]] Recommendations
The app's design prioritizes **[[Platform Partner]]s** that are geographically closer to the **[[End User]]'s** location by default. This default behavior is based on the common need to reduce travel time, minimize traffic fatigue, and save time when seeking a suitable place.
However, we understand that user needs vary, so this behavior will be fully configurable to adapt to different scenarios:

1. **Closest First:** This mode will prioritize showing the nearest suitable **[[Platform Partner]]s**, ideal for most day-to-day outings.
2. **Farthest First:** For those occasions when an **[[End User]]** might be planning a long drive or a more distant excursion, this mode will display the farthest **[[Platform Partner]]s** first.
3. **Recommendation First:** In situations where distance is less of a concern than quality or popularity, this mode will prioritize the most highly recommended **[[Platform Partner]]s**, regardless of their proximity.
