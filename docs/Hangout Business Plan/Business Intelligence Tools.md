**[[Platform Partner]]s** will receive a variety of datasets and visualizations designed to deepen their understanding of their **[[End User]]s**. This information will include details on customer likes, dislikes, demographics, and even estimated purchasing power. These insights are crucial for helping **[[Platform Partner]]s** make more informed and effective business decisions.

When **[[Platform Partner]]s** are selecting **[[Creator]]s** for promotional campaigns, they will have access to comprehensive **[[Creator]] data**. This data will include the **[[Creator]]'s** audience demographics, their audience's estimated purchasing power, engagement metrics, and a history of **[[Platform Partner]]s** they have promoted previously (if the creator chooses to make this data public). Providing these details will enable **[[Platform Partner]]s** to make strategic decisions when choosing affiliates, ensuring a better match for their promotional goals.
