## Prioritizing the [[End User Experience]]

Understanding and catering to the diverse needs of **[[End User]]s** is paramount for this platform. Every individual comes from a unique background with distinct preferences, making it crucial to grasp their specific needs and concerns before recommending any location.

When an **[[End User]]** chooses to avail a service from one of our **[[Platform Partner]]s**, it signifies a particular occasion for them. Each occasion represents an experience, and our commitment is to ensure that this **[[End User Experience]]** is as seamless and exceptional as possible.

## Leveraging LLM for [[End User]] Needs
Ultimately, **[[End User]]s** are human. We can't always articulate our precise needs perfectly, nor do we always express similar requirements using the same language. This inherent variability highlights the necessity for an **LLM (Large Language Model)**. The LLM will interpret user queries, regardless of the specific wording used, and then present relevant recommendations. This ensures that even if different words are used to describe similar needs, the recommendations provided will be consistent and accurate.
